WEAKNESSES

What Needs Improvement

CRITICAL - FIX NOW

Google Ads Are Losing Money

Your Performance Max campaign has a 1.41x ROAS. This means for every $1 spent, you get $1.41 back. After product costs (~40%), shipping, and fees, you're likely losing money on every ad-driven sale.

The Numbers:

  • $471.96 spent on ads
  • $663.46 in revenue (only 1.41x return)
  • $24.84 cost per conversion (way too high)
  • 53.74% optimization score (Google penalizing you)
  • 0.95% interaction rate (below average)

The Fix:

  1. 1. Pause all asset groups except Mailbox Decals immediately
  2. 2. Increase daily budget on mailbox-only campaign to $10/day
  3. 3. Add negative keywords: "quote decals", "wall quotes", "inspirational"
  4. 4. Fix the technical issues causing 53% optimization score
HIGH PRIORITY

Too Many Products, Not Enough Focus

You have 747+ products on Etsy alone, but the top 5% of products generate the vast majority of revenue. Many products get views but zero conversions.

High-View, Zero-Sale Products:

  • "Friends Become Family Quotes" - 69 views, 0 sales
  • "Stay Humble and Kind Wall Decal" - 58 views, 0 sales
  • "Snowman Face Decals" - 55 views, 0 sales
  • "Welcome Cursive Wall Letters" - 53 views, 0 sales
  • "Classroom Rules Subway Art" - 50 views, 0 sales

The Fix:

  1. 1. Feature top-selling categories prominently on homepage
  2. 2. Consider archiving products with 50+ views but 0 sales
  3. 3. Create dedicated landing pages for mailbox decals & growth charts
  4. 4. Stop advertising generic quote products entirely
